How it works

AS-IS to TO-BE. A methodology built for this moment.

Every engagement follows a two-stage structure. First, we establish an honest picture of where you are. Then we design and deliver the governed capability you need.

AS-IS Understand the current position
Phase 01 — Discover · Weeks 1–2

Discover

Rapid stakeholder engagement, current-state mapping, and risk identification. We surface what is already in use — what is working, what is ungoverned, and where the gaps are — so what we design reflects how your organisation actually operates.

Phase 02 — Design · Weeks 3–4

Design

Validate the AS-IS position with leadership and agree the path forward. Structured workshops align IT and business around a shared understanding of risk, appetite for change, and the first governance moves that will make the biggest practical difference.

TO-BE Build the governed capability
Phase 03 — Govern · Weeks 5–6

Govern

Establish the policies, guardrails, and accountability structures that make AI-assisted development safe at enterprise scale. Define what is approved, what requires review, and how ownership and assurance are maintained once tools move into business use.

Phase 04 — Enable · Ongoing

Enable

Stand up the Vibe Code Lab — a governed environment where business demand moves at pace through IT rather than around it. Expand adoption safely, embed governance, and continuously prioritise where AI-enabled delivery creates the most value.
