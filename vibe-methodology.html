The challenge Tactica solves

Enterprise governance has not caught up with AI-enabled development.

AI tools are moving into business use faster than IT can govern them. The risk is not the technology — it is the absence of the structures, standards, and accountability that enterprise delivery has always required. Most organisations know the gap exists. Few have a structured way to close it.

Why this is an operating model problem, not a technology problem

The tools are not the issue. The issue is that the expectations of enterprise IT — governance, ownership, assurance, lifecycle management — have not been applied to what those tools are producing. Tactica has spent over a decade applying exactly those standards to complex IT change. This is the same work, in a new context.

The Tactica difference

The methodology

AS-IS to TO-BE. Structured, bespoke, and built for this moment.

Every Vibe Code Lab engagement follows a two-stage structure built on Tactica's core methodology. First, we establish an honest, evidence-based picture of where you are. Then we design and deliver the governed capability you need. What makes it work is that every stage is bespoke — shaped around your organisation's reality, not a generic template.

Discover

Establish a fact-based picture of where your organisation actually is — not where it thinks it is. We identify your current AS-IS position, surface the key issues in play, and rapidly benchmark your organisation against peer-level companies and KPIs using Tactica's market and industry experience.

  • Stakeholder interviews to capture AI strategy as it is emerging in practice
  • Map what is currently in use or development — what is working, what is ungoverned, where the gaps are
  • Identify risks already present or emerging across visibility, control, and data
