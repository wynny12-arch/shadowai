What It Is

The Vibe Code Lab

The Vibe Code Lab is an approved, governed environment where business users collaborate directly with qualified AI development expertise to rapidly prototype, build, test, and deploy new ideas — without bypassing the controls that enterprise IT needs.

Build the capability, not the dependency

The Lab is established and run initially by Tactica specialists. From there, Tactica works alongside selected employees to transfer the skills and operating practices needed to run it independently.

The result is an organisation that owns a governed, repeatable capability — one that IT controls, the business trusts, and the organisation can scale.

1

Speed to MVP

From idea to working prototype in weeks, not months — without the procurement overhead of a traditional delivery cycle.

2

Low friction

Direct collaboration between business and IT, with governance embedded in the process rather than sitting outside it.

3

Controlled cost

Clear value prioritisation and efficient resource use — replacing fragmented local tooling and duplicated effort.

4

Safe experimentation

Ideas can be tested and validated without risk to enterprise systems, data, or compliance position.

How it's established

Tactica leads. Your people take it forward.

The Vibe Code Lab is not a consulting engagement that ends when Tactica leaves. It is a capability transfer — designed from the outset so your organisation owns and operates it independently.

No long-term dependency

Every senior manager will ask what happens after the engagement ends. The answer is that selected employees have been working alongside Tactica specialists throughout — learning the operating model, the toolstack, and the governance framework as it is built.

Tactica exits when your team is ready to run it. Not before.

2

3

Your team takes ownership

Governance at every stage

Every project moves through the same governance expectations as traditional IT delivery — but with significantly faster journey times. The Lab is designed so that rigour and speed are not a trade-off.

Ownership clarity

The governance advantage

An approved toolstack with governance built in

The Lab operates with a defined, pre-approved toolstack. Governance is not an overlay — it is part of how the environment is designed to work.

Continuous, not retrospective

Risk assessment and compliance checks happen continuously throughout development — not as a gate at the end. This removes the bottleneck without removing the control.

1

Approved toolstack

All tools are pre-approved and security-compliant. Teams work within a defined boundary — no ad hoc tool selection, no hidden risk.

2

Embedded governance

Governance is integrated into the development workflow from day one — not added afterwards when something breaks or escalates.

3

Continuous risk assessment

Risk is assessed throughout the development process, not concentrated at a single review point that creates friction and delay.

4

Compliance by design

Compliance requirements are met through the way the environment is structured — not through manual review at the end of each cycle.
